Project Infinity
← RETURN TO CORE

The Goal

To bridge the gap between biological legacy and digital persistence. By utilizing the mature CNIS architecture developed during the Bridgette phase, Project Infinity aims to map personality traits, memories, and logic patterns into a persistent AI Avatar.

The Impact

  • Perpetual Interaction: Allowing families to interact with the wisdom and personality of a loved one indefinitely.
  • Dynamic Legacy: Unlike a static video or journal, an Infinity Avatar learns and reacts as the original person would.
