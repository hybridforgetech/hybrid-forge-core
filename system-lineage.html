HybridForgeTech™ - System Lineage

The HybridForgeTech™ systems have evolved through multiple generations of AI integration:

  1. Muse: Original dual-AI core system.
  2. Bridgette: Adaptive intelligence system enhancing growth and learning.
  3. Hybrid Muse-Bridgette: Combination of Muse and Bridgette capabilities.
  4. Infinity: Blends AI with human personality and memory for hybrid intelligence.

This lineage demonstrates the growth and evolution of HybridForgeTech™ hybrid AI systems.
